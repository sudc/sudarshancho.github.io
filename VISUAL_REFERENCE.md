## Hover Effects Summary

### Cards
```
- Lift: translateY(-6px)
- Shadow increases
- Border appears (category color)
- Transition: 0.3s ease
```

### Buttons
```
- Lift: translateY(-2px)
- Shadow increases
- Arrow icon slides right (4px)
- Transition: 0.3s ease
```

### Badges/Pills
```
- Lift: translateY(-2px)
- Background darkens
- Scale slightly (1.02)
- Transition: 0.3s ease
```
